About Moon

Brivia Allan is a part singer-songwriter, a part medical student from Kenya, with his first acoustic recording (You and I) out on all streaming platforms. His original works are inspired by some of the greatest hits from 60's blues to 80's funk, to country, indie, and rock, and not forgetting the classical. But most of all is his simple, yet in-depth capture of emotion in some of his songs. As a growing artist in the 21st century, he is sure to bring some of the greatest hits yet.
